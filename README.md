AnalystLab Africa — Week 3 SQL & Business Analytics Project
Project Overview
This repository contains the data cleaning, relational database design, and advanced SQL analytical queries executed for the Week 3 project submission. The analysis spans two distinct business datasets:

Global Corporate Sales Dataset: A flat transactional log optimized to identify revenue patterns, macro performance, and inventory velocity.

Chinook Digital Music Store Database: An 11-table interconnected relational database analyzed to parse global media sales, genre trends, and artist performance metrics.

Repository Contents
global_sales_analysis.sql: Production-ready SQL queries covering date parsing, metric aggregations, geographic concentration, and window ranking functions.

chinook_music_analysis.sql: Multi-table JOIN queries extracting business insights from the relational music ecosystem.

AnalystLab_Week3_SQL_Insights_Report.docx: The final, high-impact executive brief formatted strictly to professional corporate standards.

Core Business Insights Summary
Part 1: Global Corporate Sales Key Findings
Total Revenue Generated: $10,032,628.85 across 2,823 validated transactions.

Core Product Driver: Classic Cars represents the primary revenue engine, pulling in $3,919,615.66 (nearly 40% of the entire portfolio value).

Deal Segmentation: Medium-sized contracts represent the operational sweet spot, accounting for 60.68% ($6,087,432.24) of gross revenue streams.

Market Dominance: The United States commands undisputed market leadership with a 36.16% global share ($3,627,982.83).

Part 2: Chinook Music Store Key Findings
Geographic Leaders: The United States ($523.06) and Canada ($303.96) represent the strongest geographical regions for digital music downloads.

Genre Vertical Concentration: Rock is the absolute platform pillar, capturing 835 distinct track acquisitions ($826.65)—vastly outperforming Latin and Metal.

Top Performing Catalog: Iron Maiden stands as the highest individual revenue-generating artist profile with 140 digital tracks sold ($138.60).

Technical Competencies Demonstrated
Data Sanitization & Formatting: Parsing raw text variables into native database ISO YYYY-MM-DD data structures.

Advanced Aggregations & Logic: Utilizing CASE statements, grouping matrices, and robust filtering constraints like HAVING, LIKE, and IN.

Analytical Window Functions: Deploying RANK OVER PARTITION patterns to isolate top inventory components.

Relational Multi-Table Schema Joins: Structuring deep inner data connections across independent primary and foreign key constraints.
